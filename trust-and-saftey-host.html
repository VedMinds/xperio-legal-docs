Xperio Trust & Safety Policy for Hosts

Effective Date: 01/07/2024

At Xperio, we understand the importance of creating a secure and trustworthy environment for our hosts. This policy outlines the safety measures and protocols in place to protect you while you engage with travelers and share your experiences through our platform.

1. Host Safety Guidelines

  • Secure Communication: Utilize Xperio’s in-app messaging system to communicate with guests, keeping your personal contact information private.
  • Guest Screening: Review guest profiles and their reviews before accepting bookings. Use Xperio’s platform to ask pre-booking questions if necessary.
  • Emergency Preparedness: Be prepared with emergency procedures and have a plan in place for handling unexpected situations. Keep emergency contacts handy.

2. Secure Booking and Payment System

  • Protected Transactions: All payments are securely processed through Xperio, ensuring your financial information is safe and payments are timely.
  • Payment Policy: Utilize Xperio’s payment policy to ensure you receive compensation without delay after completing an experience.

3. User Verification and Reviews

  • Traveler Verification: Benefit from Xperio's traveler verification process to enhance trust and safety for both hosts and guests.
  • Host Reviews: Encourage guests to leave reviews after their stay. Positive reviews build your reputation and attract more guests.

4. Customer Support and Incident Response

  • 24/7 Support: Xperio's support team is available around the clock for assistance with concerns or emergencies.
  • Incident Reporting: Report issues through the platform; Xperio will act to resolve conflicts and protect your safety.

5. Health and Safety Standards

Follow Xperio’s health and safety guidelines to protect yourself and your guests, especially in light of public health concerns like COVID-19.

6. Insurance and Protection

  • Host Insurance: Xperio provides insurance options to cover liabilities and damages, offering peace of mind when hosting guests.
  • Host Guarantee: Covers damage caused by guests to the host's property (e.g., broken furniture or damaged flooring).
  • Host Protection Insurance: Covers liability claims made by guests or third parties for injuries or property damage during their stay.
  • Experiences Liability Insurance: Provides up to ₹5 lakh in coverage for claims related to guest injuries or property damage during Xperio Experiences.

7. Legal and Regulatory Compliance

Stay informed about local laws that affect hosting activities. Xperio offers resources and support to help you comply with legal requirements.

8. Feedback and Continuous Improvement

We value your feedback and encourage you to share your hosting experiences to help us improve our services and policies.

9. Updates to Policy

This Trust & Safety policy is reviewed and updated regularly to reflect the latest security measures, technological advancements, and host feedback.

10. Contact Us

For additional inquiries or immediate support, please contact our dedicated host support team at support@xperio.travel.
